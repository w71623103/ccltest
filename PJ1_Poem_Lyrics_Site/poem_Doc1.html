Yinqi Wang

Nothing is coming into my mind.
At the time when I need to write.
My mind is empty and my eyes are blind.

Why not write about beauty of mankind?
But I cannot think of a theme that is right.
Nothing is coming into my mind.

How about a land that is only in your mind?
But the way towards there was already blight.
My mind is empty and my eyes are blind.

Music of words is hiding inside my head,
Refusing me while I’m struggling for a rhyme.
Nothing is coming into my mind.

Deadline is approaching to its time.
With the clock running towards late night.
My mind is empty and my eyes are blind.

Starting from when, writing becomes like a grind?
I’m throwing words out, one by one in byte.
Nothing is coming into my mind.
My mind is empty and my eyes are blind.
